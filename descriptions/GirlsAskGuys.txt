GirlsAskGuys is the digital community of trusted and anonymous friends where girls and guys help each other by sharing their experiences & opinions. GirlsAskGuys connects over 12 million people per month to help each other in 3 languages; GirlsAskGuys.com(English), KizlarSoruyor.com (Turkish) & EllasSaben.com (Spanish).

Our community provides experienced, trusted and anonymous best friends from the opposite sex, who users feel safe and comfortable asking for feedback and opinions, no matter if it’s personal, embarrassing or just something they’re curious about, in a safe and non-judgmental environment.

KizlarSoruyor.com, the Turkish version of GirlsAskGuys.com, launched in 2011 and in 2014, the Spanish version, EllasSaben.com, went live.
