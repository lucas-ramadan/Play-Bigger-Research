Viptela provides technology for virtualization of the Wide Area Network (WAN). It allows global companies to build carrier agnostic, policy-controlled and cost-effective WANs. Its technology has been deployed by scores of Fortune-500 enterprises. And, major carriers like Singtel offer global Hybrid WAN services based on Viptela technology.

Viptela’s secure, overlay WAN technology delivers to enterprises the benefit of:
• Unified and centralized management of all WAN infrastructure
• Reduced WAN infrastructure costs by over 50%
• In-depth control & visibility of application performance
• Robust-zero trust WAN security
• Agility, by reducing network change control from months to days
