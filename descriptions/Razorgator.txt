RazorGator is the 3rd largest player in the online ticket reselling business behind eBay's [StubHub](http://www.stubhub.com) and Ticketmaster's [TicketsNow](http://www.crunchbase.com/company/ticketsnow) according to December 2007 comScore data.  The service covers reselling of sports, theater, and concert tickets, as well as vacation packages for various sporting events.
