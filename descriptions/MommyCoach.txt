MommyCoach is creating the first Live Video SaaS platform allowing independant coaches & experts to take their practice to the digital world, therefore boosting their business and giving them access 100,000's of new potential clients nationwide. Through their consumer site mommycoach.com, they are building a marketplace of coaches & certified experts that will allow all mothers to access them at an affordable price and using a high quality live video experience. 

Based in New York City, MommyCoach is funded by its founder & Spark Labs as well as a group of first-class angel investors including White Start Capital, Start Capital, Mark Kingdon, Allen Morgan and Jean-Marie Messier.
