Age of Learning, Inc., is the educational technology and digital learning company that created and continues to develop ABCmouse.com. At Age of Learning our goal is to help children build a strong foundation for future academic success by providing a comprehensive and engaging online and app-based curriculum to greatly assist early learners to succeed in pre-k, kindergarten, and early elementary school programs. ABCmouse.com offers monthly and yearly subscriptions for home use and offers free classroom accounts to public and private schools, Head Start Centers, libraries and other community organizations.
