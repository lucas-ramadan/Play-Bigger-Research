PathCentral, an Irvine, California-based provider of a fully integrated, web-based enterprise software platform designed specifically for community pathologists. Created by a team of pathologists and industry executives, PathCentral's solution integrates all aspects of a pathology practice's business, including ordering (accessioning), barcode-based laboratory workflow management and specimen tracking, transcription, test send-outs, professional consultations, diagnosis, reporting, billing and financial analysis.
