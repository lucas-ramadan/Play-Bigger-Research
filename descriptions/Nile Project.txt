The Nile Project, Inc. provides online recommendations for people looking to explore popular travel destinations around the world. The company offers various travel recommendations and itineraries covering what to see and do, as well as where to stay and eat in various popular travel destinations. It also provides travel articles, guides, and advisory support, as well as facilitates travelers with the ability to ask questions and get answers from various locals. The company was founded in 2006 and is based in San Francisco, California. As of April 24, 2012, The Nile Project, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Travora Media, Inc.
