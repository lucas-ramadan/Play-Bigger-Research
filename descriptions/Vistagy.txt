VISTAGY, Inc. develops and markets engineering software that turns commercial 3D CAD systems into specialized engineering environments. It offers EnCapta technology that provides specialized engineering environments for aero structures development, airframe design and assembly, composites engineering, and seating/interiors design and manufacturing. The company also provides solutions in the areas of automated deposition design, documentation, analysis interface, flat pattern export, laser projection, fiber placement interface, tape laying interface, and composite viewer, as well as airframe design environment, airframe manufacturing environment, and quality planning environment. In addition, it offers process audits, practices, training, and implementation services. The company serves various industries, including aerospace and defense, automotive, marine, space, medical equipment, transportation interiors, recreational products, consumer goods, and wind energy. The company has a strategic partnership with Delta Sigma Corporation. VISTAGY, Inc. was formerly known as Composite Design Technologies, Inc. and changed its name to VISTAGY, Inc. in January 2001. The company was founded in 1991 and is based in Waltham, Massachusetts with additional offices in Dallas and Fort Worth, Texas; London, England; Paris, France; Munich and Stuttgart, Germany; Beijing, China; Tokyo, Japan; and Changwon, South Korea.
