GotVoice creates digital voice applications that allow consumers and businesses to unify their voice communications through their email, mobile phones, or via the GotVoice Website. 

The features of its products include Voicemail-to-Text, Visual Voicemail, the ability to share voice messages as digital audio files, and the unification of wireless, landline, PBX and VoIP voicemail.

Founded in 2003 by industry executives from [RealNetworks](http://www.crunchbase.com/company/realnetworks), [Microsoft](http://www.crunchbase.com/company/microsoft), Starwave, AT&T and Corbis, GotVoice works with all major mobile, business and residential carriers.
