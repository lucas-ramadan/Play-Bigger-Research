Pricelock’s online platform provides buyers and sellers an easy and efficient way to transact energy and energy-related products. Pricelock’s mission is to provide liquidity for products and services that cannot be traded on an exchange. It also offers risk management services and an employee incentive product.

Pricelock’s investors include Goldman Sachs, Barclays, Traveler’s Insurance, RenaissanceRe, and Artiman Ventures. To learn more about Pricelock, please visit www.pricelock.com.
