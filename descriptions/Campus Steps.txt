Campus Steps is reinventing college counseling for everyone. Campus Steps' mobile and web solutions offer on-demand counseling services and media to 'step' students through every phase of their college readiness; from middle and high school course planning, to college selection, to applications and organizing finances. Along the way students connect with counselors and admissions professionals plus get data-driven insights to make the decision-making process less stressful and more communicative. Campus Steps is headquartered in Santa Monica, CA and is a proud member of the Silicon Beach community.
