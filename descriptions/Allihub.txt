Allihub, Inc., doing business as Homejoy Inc., operates an online platform that connects households with service providers-starting with home cleaning in the United States and Canada. It provides cleaning solutions for bedrooms, living rooms, bathrooms, and kitchens; and customizes cleaning services with laundry, dishwashing, ovens, refrigerators, cabinets, windows, and wall cleaning. The company was incorporated in 2010 and is based in San Francisco, California.
