Bitzer Mobile, Inc. is a provider of infrastructure software for enterprises to use on-premise or as part of cloud computing environments. CIOs and IT managers use Bitzer to securely extend enterprise application access on mobile devices and support consumerization by allowing employees to use their personally purchased device in the enterprise. Bitzer technology has been under development since mid- 2009. The company was founded in mid-2010. Bitzer is located in the heart of Silicon Valley in Sunnyvale, CA.
