GetMyBoat is changing the way the world goes boating by powering the largest boat rental marketplace. With over 45,000 boats in 154 countries and 5,300 locations, there is an experience for new boaters and seasoned sailors alike. Launched in San Francisco in 2013, GetMyBoat empowers owners of every type of watercraft, from kayaks and sailboats to powerboats and yachts, to post their watercraft for rent or charter. From California to Croatia, our users can search, find and book their dreamboat. Go Ahead, Make Waves.
