Stockr is a social media platform for the financial world that connects investors directly to the people and companies they want to follow.

July 2012 Private Beta release features included:

- Pulse: a real-time feed of system wide chatter aggregated by ticker symbol

- News: real-time news feeds from almost 200 original news sources, that users can customize to stream only the news that is most valuable to them 

- Investor Profile Pages: basic user information and what stocks the users follow

- Company Pages: a broad spectrum of historical data and interactive charts, along with real-time company news and system chatter

- Watch Lists: users can create portfolios and choose to share them with other users
