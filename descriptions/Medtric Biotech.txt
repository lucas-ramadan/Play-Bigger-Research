Medtric aims to provide the measurable difference by delivering clinically robust and economically sensible wound care solutions. They formed the company with this simple vision in mind. Focus on Innovation: Their company's focus is on developing innovative products to advance patient care. Medtric's flagship technology was developed collaboratively at leading research and medical institutions. Their nanoscale-based therapy was specifically engineered to protect wounds against infection and advance the healing process. Strategy for Growth: As they look to the future, their strategy is to enter the expanding clinical and over the counter wound care markets. Medtric will also leverage this platform to penetrate alternative applications inculding the treatment of acne and other topical infections. Their strategy ensures a strong foundation with expansive capabilities.
