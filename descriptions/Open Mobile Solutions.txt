Open Mobile Solutions provides a web-based business-to-business marketplace that helps decision makers find qualified resources to develop, test, port, monetize, distribute and analyze mobile applications for smartphones, feature phones and tablet devices.

The OMS web marketplace matches application creators with firms skilled in one or more areas of the mobile application lifecycle and performs analytics to create a profile of customer choices and project results. You can easily submit a single request for proposal to multiple OMS-qualified firms you select.
