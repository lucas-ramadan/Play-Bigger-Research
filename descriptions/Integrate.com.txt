Integrate’s cloud-based marketing platform empowers marketers to more effectively acquire prospects and customers by integrating media programs and prospect data with existing marketing and sales systems. The closed-loop platform provides tools including workflow automation, data governance, analytics and a high-quality media partner marketplace, all accessed from a single, intuitive dashboard. Integrate’s solution efficiently fuels marketing systems with prospects and actionable data and closes the loop to increase performance and measure ROI. Serving more than 2,500 customers and 3,500 media partners, Integrate is backed by Comcast Ventures, Liberty Global Ventures and Foundry Group.
