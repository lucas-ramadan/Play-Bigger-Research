Proxicom is an interactive agency that develops custom-tailored interactive and web-enabled solutions for Global 1000 organizations.


The company services industries such as automotive, financial services, retail and consumer goods, healthcare, technology and media, government, energy and utilities, and industrial materials and goods.


Proxicom was brought by Dimension Data in 2001, The Gores Group in 2004, and currently it operates under Hearst Corporation, which brought the company in 2007.


Proxicom was founded by Raul Fernandez in 1991.
