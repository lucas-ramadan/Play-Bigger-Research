OrderGroove's mission is revolutionizing the way people shop, one relationship at a time. Launched in September 2010, OrderGroove's SaaS-based subscription technology platform and network is the world's first for B2C businesses and CMOs, enabling major brands to develop relationships with their most valuable customers via the subscription model — taking customer loyalty to exclusivity, while dramatically increasing annual spend and lifetime value by 2-4x.  OrderGroove works with many blue chip brands including Walmart, CVS, L’Oreal, Procter and Gamble, Grainger, Jarden as well as General Electric. The company is privately held, headquartered in New York City, and backed by investors including Lerer Hippeau Ventures, Allegro VC and SWaN and Legend Ventures.
