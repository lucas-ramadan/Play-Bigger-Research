npm is the official package manager for [Node.js](http://www.crunchbase.com/product/node-js). npm, Inc. supports the JavaScript community by providing the registry where developers publish and share packaged open-source modules.

npm, Inc. is led by Isaac Z. Schlueter (“isaacs of Node”, creator of npm, former Node.js BDFW), Laurie Voss (Yahoo!, Awe.sm, Chief Technical Seldo), and Rod Boothby (Joyent, Ernst & Young, Wells Fargo, Chief Operational Business Person).
