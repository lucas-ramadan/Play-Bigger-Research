Bump Technologies builds mobile applications and APIs that allow two smartphones to identify one another and connect by simply being bumped together.

The company was founded in 2008 and is based in Mountain View, California. Bump Technologies is funded by [Y Combinator](/company/y-combinator), [Sequoia Capital](/financial-organization/sequoia-capital), [Andreessen Horowitz](/financial-organization/andreessen-horowitz) and other angel investors.
