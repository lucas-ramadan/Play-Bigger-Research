Fundrise is the leading online real estate investment crowdfunding platform. Starting in 2012, Fundrise was the first company to take commercial real estate public online and offer true equity ownership in local properties.

Fundrise offers real estate investments for both accredited and unaccredited investors, and allows real estate companies to build their investment network and raise investment online through a full service, web-based platform. 

Fundrise reduces the costs associated with traditional real estate investment by cutting out unnecessary middlemen and making the process more efficient.  This innovative new model makes the process of investing in real estate simple and more cost-effective, ultimately driving higher returns for investors.  Fundrise focuses on working with the best real estate companies in major metro markets, providing attractive investment opportunities across the country.
