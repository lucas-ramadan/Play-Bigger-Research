Gnip is the world’s largest and most trusted provider of social data. Gnip was the first to partner with Twitter to make their social data available, and since then have been the first to work with Tumblr, Foursquare, WordPress, Disqus, StockTwits and more.

Gnip delivers social data to customers in more than 40 countries, and Gnip’s customers deliver social media analytics to more than 95% of the Fortune 500.

Gnip is headquartered in Boulder, Colo., and has offices in San Francisco, New York and Washington D.C.
