Intronis Technologies specializes in data backup for the entire range of consumers, SMBs and large enterprises. Intronis' eSureIT online backup service securely automates the process of offsite data backup with two distinct mirrored storage facilities. Data is fully encrypted utilizing a unique 256-bit encryption key with full file restoration possible from any location at any time.

Intronis, LLC was founded in 2003 and is based in Boston, Massachusetts.
