AltaSens, Inc. designs and develops high definition (HD) and wide dynamic range complementary metal oxide semiconductors image sensors. The company offers color or monochrome HD sensors, and sensor comparison matrix products. Its sensors are used by camera manufacturers for various applications in videoconference and telepresence, surveillance, broadcast and cinematography, medical, and industrial markets. The company serves customers in Asia, Europe, and North America. The company was incorporated in 2003 and is headquartered in Westlake Village, California with additional offices or distributors in North America, Taiwan, Japan, China, Europe, and Israel. AltaSens, Inc. As of August 10, 2012, AltaSens, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of JVC KENWOOD Corporation.
