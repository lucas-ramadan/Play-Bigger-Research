KISI is a smartphone-enabled access control system. Designed for businesses, commercial office and residential buildings, KISI integrates with existing infrastructure to add a whole new level of speed, convenience and functionality to traditional access control systems. The system consists of a beautifully designed, one-device-fits-all gateway, an easy-to-use online management system and the KISI App. With KISI, key cards and fobs can be a thing of the past.
