Shape Security stops automated web and mobile application attacks, like credential stuffing, content scraping, and application-­layer DDoS, that bypass existing defenses. The Shape Botwall Service, available in the cloud or on­-premises, protects more than 350M accounts on behalf of Fortune 500 organizations. Invisible to people but impenetrable to bots, Shape protects the world’s largest banks, airlines, retailers and government agencies. Shape Security is headquartered in Silicon Valley and backed by Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, Norwest Venture Partners, Venrock, Baseline Ventures, Google Ventures, and a host of other prominent investors.
