The Shelf combines big data with machine learning enabling brands to fully harness the power of Influencers. 

At it’s core, The Shelf’s patent-pending Global Influence Graph captures the deep connections between influencers, brands, products, purchases, and consumers. These connections are the secret sauce to fully capture the power of influencers. 

By making these connections queryable and flexible, The Shelf makes it easy to implement multiple customer-facing applications, such as:
--ROI-Driven Influencer Marketing,
--E-Commerce Analytics,
--Trend Prediction,
--Loyalty Program,
--Social Listening,
--Behavioral Targeting for Advertising.


TheShelf was part of **[AngelPad](http://angelpad.org) #6** in 2013.
