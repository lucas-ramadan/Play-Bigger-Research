Workboard provides a free app for managers to manage efficiently and lead brilliantly.It simplifies how teams set goals and priorities, delegate and share the work to achieve them, get transparency and status reports, and give feedback on accomplishments.

The company was founded by serial entrepreneurs, Deidre Paknad and Daryoush Paknad.Deidre's last company created and led a new enterprise market category; it was acquired by IBM in 2010 where she went on to lead a fast-growing global business.The Workboard leadership team knows first hand the challenges global and enterprise managers and teams face to stay aligned and deliver stellar results and has long-lasting relationships with large enterprise clients.
