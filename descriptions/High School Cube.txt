The Cube (http://thecube.com) is a live streaming app that makes it simple to live stream and share moments - LIVE. Specifically, The Cube provides a platform for schools, businesses, organization and individuals to stream their events LIVE, cut highlight clips, create mixes, interact and share.  The Cube is now used by more than 4,000 high schools across 50 states and 17 countries to showcase more than 1.2 million people LIVE to a growing audience of 20 million family members, friends and fans. 

The Cube was founded by an experienced exec team who have 4 successful startups under their belt; apartments.com, apartmenthomeliving.com, sandbox, and auto.com.  The Cube was founded in Chicago but now calls San Francisco home with offices also in Chicago.
