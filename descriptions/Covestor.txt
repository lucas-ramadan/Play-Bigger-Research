Covestor brings the clarity and efficiency of an online marketplace to the investment management industry. Investors can compare and select from a transparent marketplace of investment management talent that includes top performing portfolio managers and successful investors. Covestor’s Portfolio Sync technology automatically replicates trades, providing clients the convenience of “set and forget” functionality and the protection of Covestor’s proprietary trade filtering. 

Covestor is widely regarded as a pioneer in bringing separately managed accounts (SMAs) online in a transparent marketplace. The company has offices in Boston and London.
