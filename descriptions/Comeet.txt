Comeet is the collaborative recruiting platform that transforms your hiring team into a dream team, making smarter, faster hires. From streamlining the entire process and providing you on-the-go integration of recruitment’s ‘best practices’, you’ll be using our slick hiring techniques and tools like a pro - even if you’re no hiring expert. 
Who uses Comeet? 

Recruiters and office admins use it to manage the candidate pipeline, delegate tasks and interviews to team members and track hiring health. 

Hiring managers and interviewers use it for preparing and conducting interviews, submitting feedback and staying on top of the process.
