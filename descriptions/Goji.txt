Goji has developed a smart lock that gives users complete control over access to their homes using their mobile phones. 

The Goji Smart Lock is distinguished by its sleek, sophisticated design and its unique ability to send you real-time picture alerts via text or email of the individuals accessing your home.

The Goji Smart Lock presents a contemporary new look for a deadbolt lock: a sleek, modern disc in brushed metal with a subtle blue-text LED display that welcomes users by name when the lock is activated. The display also shows the lock/unlock status, and dates and times of all entries and exits.
