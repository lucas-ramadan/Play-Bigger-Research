EvntLive, a digital concert venue, streams live and on-demand concerts of artists to users’ computer, tablet, and mobile devices.
 It lets its users to watch a sold out concert, discover new artists, and share its users’ live music experiences with their friends.

 
 EvntLive provides its users an All Access pass to the shows, allowing them to learn the story behind its users’ favorite artists while enjoying the same songs live.

 
 EvntLive is based in Redwood City, C.A.
