Taggstr enables you to make your world more "taggable" and leave your own marks on places around the world.

Through rich content in the form of messages, pictures, and videos, Taggstr aims to create new communities around the interesting places that people visit. It is an easy way to "tagg" and share peoples' favorite places directly from their phone. 

Taggstr released their iPhone and Android applications in August of 2010.
