Digg Inc. operates a website that enables its users to find, read, and share the most interesting and talked about stories on the internet. It is an editorially driven front page, aiming to select stories related to science, politics, and viral internet issues.

 
 It also enables users to find stories on their iOS or Android devices through its mobile apps; and delivers stories via daily emails.

 
 Digg Inc. was founded in 2004 and is based in San Francisco, California. Currently, it operates as a subsidiary of News.me, Inc.
