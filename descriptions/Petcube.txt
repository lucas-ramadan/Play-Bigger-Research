Petcube is a device that enables its users to play with their pets in real time over the internet. It is a home gadget that lets users watch, talk, and play laser games with their pets through its mobile application.


Petcube features a wide-angle camera, a microphone, a speaker and a laser toy. Its mobile application enables users to point a laser pointer around spaces for their pets to follow. People can also share photos and videos of pets taken with their Petcube and share access to their devices. So people who don't have pets or Petcube can download a free app and play with other people's pets.


Petcube was founded by Andrey Klen, Yaroslav Azhnyuk, and Alex Neskin in May 2012 and is based in San Francisco, California, United States. The company was funded by Kickstarter.
