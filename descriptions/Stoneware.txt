Stoneware, Inc. provides a software solution that enables organizations to build their own private cloud to access their applications and services. It offers webNetwork, a private cloud computing framework that helps organizations in transition from a client-centric environment to a centralized Web computing environment. The company also provides technical support services; and training for beginning webNetwork administrators, advanced system managers, and product developers. It serves various sectors, including education, manufacturing, healthcare, legal, and financial industries. Stoneware, Inc. was incorporated in 2000 and is based in Carmel, Indiana. It has locations in Fort Wayne, Indiana; and Orem, Utah.
