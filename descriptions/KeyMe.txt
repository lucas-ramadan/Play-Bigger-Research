KeyMe is a New York City-based startup that is revolutionizing the way people manage their keys. It allows users to store, share, and duplicate their physical keys based on a digital image.  Full automated, self service key duplicating kiosks can be found in 7-Eleven, Bed Bath & Beyond, and Rite Aid stores.  The free mobile app is available in the iTunes store and at key.me/download.
