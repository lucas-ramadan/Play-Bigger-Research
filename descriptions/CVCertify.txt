Acertiv is like Carfax for Careers. 

Acertiv.com is an online work history certification platform that integrates with existing workflows to find qualified candidates faster and cheaper.  Candidates use professional social networks and other tools to build ProofSheets, validating specific resume claims at the bullet point level.  Certified claims are then mapped to specific employer requirements. 

Enterprises license Acertiv.com to filter candidates applying to job postings, efficiently identifying proven candidates and reducing process cycle time.  Candidates enjoy a permanent repository to manage and re-use certified profile data. 

Proven Candidates.  Certified Careers.
