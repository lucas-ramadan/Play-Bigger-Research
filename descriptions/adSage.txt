AdSage (艾德思奇) is a digital marketing agency offering search engine optimization and advertising solutions. It was founded in 2007, with offices in both China and America.

The flagship product of adSage is adSage for Search (AFS), a professional delivery and automation software tool set. AFS helps advertisers effectively manage and optimize the online advertising, and at the same time, it crosses different search engines. AdSage mobile advertising platform (mobiSage) and adSage mobile advertising aggregation platform, which is called mobiSage mediator for short, provide accurate intelligent marketing platform for extensive advertisers and developers to enjoy unlimited mobile marketing value.
