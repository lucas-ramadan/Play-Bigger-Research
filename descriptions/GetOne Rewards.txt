GetOne Rewards LLC develops software and mobile applications for retail sectors. The company provides digitalizes traditional loyalty card system into social media, mobile communication, and marketing engine platform. It offers digital loyalty, mobile communication, mobile marketing, and tracking and reporting solutions. Its digital platform solution enables businesses to track and measure their rewards program; communicate and market to their customers; and send personalized messages and coupons to their mobile phones. GetOne Rewards LLC was formerly known as Enoteg LLC and changed its name to GetOne Rewards LLC in November 2012.
