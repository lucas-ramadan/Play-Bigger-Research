Intent Media (www.intentmedia.com) offers an end-to-end solution for commerce sites seeking to maximize the profitability of their site traffic.  Drawing heavily on the company's expertise in data science and intent targeting, Intent Media helps retailers safely extract both the transaction and advertising value of their visitors.  The company's backers include Matrix Partners and Redpoint Ventures, LinkedIn co-Founder Reid Hoffman, AppNexus CEO Brian O'Kelley, and Gilt Groupe CEO Kevin Ryan.
