The world is shrinking. Global travel is the norm and nearly everyone has a mobile device, or two, or three. In the enterprise, the challenges to maintain the highest productivity levels at the most cost-effective levels is daunting.

Today, mobile connectivity for the globally roaming “out of network” workforce is expensive, but still mission critical. The mobile enterprise workforce today has to deal with data roaming that is very expensive, but the alternative for those with the techy expertise the DiY is too complicated and time consuming. All of this is exacerbated by the need to connect multiple devices, the smart phone, laptop, and now tablet.

To keep up with the demands of a global mobile workforce the enterprise needs a new standard. Introducing Gigsky.

Simply put, using the Gigsky App with your mobile router, will allow users to access a local prepaid data service in the visited country, at local rates.
