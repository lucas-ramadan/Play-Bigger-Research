ClubcastLIVE is the leading event to event live broadcast platform, helping brands cost effectively scale their experiential initiatives through a fully integrated platform. With its proprietary multi-way, HD live streaming software companies can now interactively connect events in 1 location to many other locations. The company supports customers across 3 verticals: Experiential Marketing, Training and Corporate Events/Conferences.

The company is based in Los Angeles and is Series A-backed.
