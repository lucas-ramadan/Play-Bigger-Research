OwnLocal monetizes digital advertising revenue for local media companies by using technology to automatically transform traditional print, radio and TV ads to comprehensive search-engine optimized digital marketing campaigns. 

These campaigns include: ad-landing pages, SEO websites, Google SEM, social banner ads, social-media marketing, and almost anything else via OwnLocal's own API. 

The conversion and sales are all done automatically, instantly producing all new revenue for local newspapers and publishers. OwnLocal does everything from educating local ad reps on how to package and sell this technology, to fulfilling all orders, and covering support. 

OwnLocal works with more than 2,000+ local publishers, 53,000+ local small businesses, and their software serves more than a billion impressions per month.
