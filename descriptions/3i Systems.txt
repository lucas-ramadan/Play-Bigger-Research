3i Systems Corporation (中导光电设备科技有限公司) is an entrepreneurial firm focused on large-scale optoelectronic equipments. Its products are used in panel display and photovoltaic industries. 3i Systems Corporation has the complete independent intellectual property which includes scientific and technical invention patents and software copyrights domestically and overseas, covering  over 20 items in total.

It has a core technical team made up of experts in semiconductor equipment. Of the employees of 3i Systems Corporation, 18% of the employees of 3i Systems Corporation have doctor's degrees or senior professional titles, and 45% have master's degrees or medium professional titles. 

3i Systems Corporation was Founded in November, 2006.
