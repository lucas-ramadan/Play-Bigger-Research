Campus Special is the industry leader for print, mobile, and online marketing solutions for local businesses looking to target college students.  Originally a print advertising company, Campus Special has evolved into a digital consumer brand for college students and provides small businesses with online and mobile SaaS platforms to target the 18-24 year old demographic.  The company has been ranked by Inc. Magazine as one of the fastest growing private companies in the US in 2010, 2011, 2012, and 2013.  Campus Special currently reaches millions of students at 500+ college campuses and services over 17,000 local merchants nationwide.
