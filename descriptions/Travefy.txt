Travefy is the easiest online and mobile group travel planner. Through Travefy, groups can collaborate on trip details, build rich itineraries, and collect shared expenses so no one is stuck with the bill.

Travefy is available directly to consumers and branded Pro versions are available for travel brands, suppliers, and agents among others. Travefy has been featured in The New York Times, Forbes, Fast Company, and U.S. News and World Report among other publications and has won numerous innovation awards.
