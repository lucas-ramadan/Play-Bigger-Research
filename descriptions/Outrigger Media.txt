Outrigger Media Inc. is a leading online video advertising company focused on connecting brands with meaningful video content. Since 2009, the company has partnered with a wide range of professional producers and aggregators, as well as with some of the biggest marketers in the world. The company's focus on developing better contextual relevance between video content and campaign messages has led to the development of OpenSlate Studios, a data and marketplace platform connecting innovative brands and qualified producers at scale. With OpenSlate, Outrigger is introducing a new measure for valuing independent video content. For more information, visit www.OpenSlateStudios.com.
