Terapeak is the leader in ecommerce analytics.  The Terapeak  platform provides historical and current marketplace transactional data, to help online retailers better understand product, pricing, competitor and market behavior. The Terapeak solution gives online retailers a multi-dimensional view  of the eBay and Amazon marketplace, providing them with deep market insights to help mitigate risk and aid in the decision making process.

The Terapeak platform analyzes over 4.6 billion transactions per year and over $70 Billion in online consumer spending worldwide. Online sellers have proven to be, on average, 65% more successful through the use of Terapeak's analytical, query-based data.
