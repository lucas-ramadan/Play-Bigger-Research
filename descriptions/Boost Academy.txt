Boost Academy™ offers a private, one-on-one, tablet-based tutoring service. Fully-interactive, cost-effective and easy to use, Boost Academy transforms the tutoring experience from today’s inefficient traditional model to a digital platform delivered via the iPad® (Android coming soon), eliminating time zone and geograpny barriers to deliver learning when and where it is needed. 

All lessons are live, voice-enabled and recorded (voice, text chat and white board screens) with playback feature for test prep, progress-monitoring and quality control. Now,successful learning is within reach for any student with access to a tablet and the internet using Boost Academy’s robust application to improve confidence, grades and test results and to foster college and career-readiness in today’s competitive, global economy.
