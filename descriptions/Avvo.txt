Avvo is the largest online legal marketplace connecting consumers and lawyers. In Avvo’s Q&A forum, consumers can get their legal questions answered for free by more than 175,000 participating lawyers or search more than 6 million previously posted questions and lawyer-provided answers. The lawyer directory provides Avvo-rated profiles, client reviews, and peer endorsements for 97 percent of all lawyers in the U.S., so consumers can find the lawyer who’s right for them. 

Avvo also helps lawyers grow their businesses with marketing tools and services including Avvo Ignite, a cloud-based marketing platform designed specifically for attorneys.

Avvo launched in 2007 and is headquartered in Seattle, WA. For more information, visit www.avvo.com.
