Atmospheir is a social address book and CRM that provides the fastest way to discover, connect and communicate with your contacts. Create a personalized ID and share it with friends to connect and exchange as much contact and social network information as you want. We'll notify you and update your address book whenever your contacts update their information. Enjoy up-to-date and complete information for every contact and and free text, voice, photo, GIF and location messaging with everyone you connect with in the app.
