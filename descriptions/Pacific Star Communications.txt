Pacific Star Communications Inc. develops and delivers information technology and communications solutions to military, government, and commercial sectors. The company offers wireless networking, communication unification and deployment, switching and routing, network security, and voice integration solutions. Additionally, it provides telecommunications and broadband solutions such as premise, central office, and outside plant equipment. The company markets its products under PacStar 6300, PacStar 6800, PacStar 7000, and IQ-Core brand names. It has strategic alliances with World Wide Technology, Inc. and INX Inc. Pacific Star Communications Inc. was founded in 2000 and is based in Portland, Oregon.
