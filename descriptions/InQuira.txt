InQuira, Inc. provides a software platform that includes knowledge base management, natural language search, and analytics and reporting applications. Its products include Intelligent Search, a semantic search system for understanding and responding to user inquiries; Information Manager, a knowledge management application for identifying, developing, organizing, and sharing enterprise information; and Analytics, a reporting application for managing user experience on the Web and in contact centers. 

The company was founded in 2002 and is based in San Bruno, California.

InQuira was acquired by Oracle in July 2011.
