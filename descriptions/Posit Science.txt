Posit Science Corporation designs and tests clinically validated computer-based brain fitness programs. Its programs focus on the brain's various systems and target the roots of memory and thinking. The company offers online brain training exercises in a program called BrainHQ as well as a driving-focused program called DriveSharp. Its programs address cognitive issues related to healthy aging, Alzheimer's disease, mild cognitive impairment, Parkinson's, chronic pain, schizophrenia, chemobrain, among other conditions. Posit Science Corporation was formerly known as Neuroscience Solutions Corporation and changed its name in January, 2005. The company was founded in 2002 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California.
