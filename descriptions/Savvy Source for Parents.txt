The Savvy Source for Parents, Inc. operates an online community that provides information, ratings, and reviews on preschools, events, places, and camps in San Francisco. The company enables users to create and join groups; follow their favorite parenting bloggers; publish updates and a family journal; share favorite books, toys, and gear; find reviews on kid-friendly products; and more. It also allows parents to communicate on various topics and share information with their friends; and provides local information about fun things to do with kids in various cities, as well as parenting tips. The company was incorporated in 2005 and is based in San Francisco, California.
