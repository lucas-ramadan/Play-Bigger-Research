Quri is a retail intelligence company that provides instant visibility into the performance of products and promotions in retail stores.  Quri enables brands and retailers to measure and optimize the exact shopper experience, maximizing in-store product revenue and profitability.  Quri is powered by a nationwide, on-demand mobile field force of secret shoppers who can be immediately deployed to any retail location in the U.S., providing real-time actionable data and insights to brands and retailers. The company is based in San Francisco, CA.  For more information please visit: http://www.quri.com.
