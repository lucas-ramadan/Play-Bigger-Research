Wikia is an online platform that provides information related to several categories. It provides its users with information and reviews related to console and mobile games, cable and streaming TV shows, music festivals, big movie franchises, major sporting events, anticipated book releases, fashion trends and DIY, food and drink recipes, and current events on an international scale.


It provides its information under seven segments: games, movies, TV, comics, music, books, and lifestyle.


Wikia was founded in 2006 by Angela Beesley and Jimmy Wales. It has more than 200 employees worldwide and is headquartered in San Francisco, with offices in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, London, Cologne, Tokyo, and Poznan, Poland.
