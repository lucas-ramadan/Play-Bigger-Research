StarMaker is an online video network whose mission is to move content creation and talent discovery from traditional media to the mobile and social space. Combining the power of content creation apps, recording studio technology, crowd-sourced talent discovery and a huge network of user channels on YouTube, StarMaker's platform enables artists to manage their career without the antiquated Entertainment Industry waste.  StarMaker also integrates major media brands and recording artists, helping our Talent create, share, discover and promote each other's user-generated media.
