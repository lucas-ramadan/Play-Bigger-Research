[PurePlay](http://player.pureplay.com/) is a play-for-cash
online poker game.  Through a patented business model, PurePlay
enables players to compete for cash without putting their own money at risk.  Players compete by paying monthly fee.  The company was founded in November 2004.  As of May 2008,  PurePlay has awarded over $3 million and has over 1,000,000 players.
