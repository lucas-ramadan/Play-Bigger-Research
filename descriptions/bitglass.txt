As enterprises adopt SaaS applications and users go mobile, IT is faced with securing corporate data that resides on third-party servers and travels over third-party networks to mobile devices. Conventional security technologies are not suited to the task as they were developed to secure the corporate network perimeter. Enter Bitglass.

Bitglass was founded in 2013 by a team of industry veterans with a proven track record of innovation and execution. Bitglass has attracted investment from Tier 1 venture capital firms Norwest Venture Partners and NEA, via $10M Series A investment in February 2013. Bitglass is based in Silicon Valley.
