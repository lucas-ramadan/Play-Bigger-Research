[Hukkster](http://hukkster.com) is an online shopping tool that tracks products on the sites you love and notifies you when they go on sale. In one simple step, the Hukkster bookmarklet installs in your browser bar and acts as an ever-present companion that organizes and optimizes your online shipping. With Hukkster, shoppers can liberate their inboxes from the clutter of daily deals and generic promotions and only be notified about products they actually want.
