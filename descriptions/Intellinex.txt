Intellinex is a U.S.-based company that provides customized learning solutions to help with business strategies. Intellinex provided dynamic learning services to selected global organizations such as Ernst & Young, Cisco Systems, Canon, Wireless Retail, Oce Technologies, and Turner Construction.
 
The company is now considered a leader in learning process outsourcing services. 
 
Intellinex was founded in 1999 and is a subsidiary of Xerox.
