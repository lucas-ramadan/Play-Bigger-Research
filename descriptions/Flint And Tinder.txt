Flint And Tinder USA, LLC, a clothing company, designs and manufactures men's underwear. The company offers basic, trunk, and boxer briefs; and T-shirts, shaving creams and brushes, field archery wall art products, denim products, dock-line rope leash products, trap belts, summer board shorts, rosewood barlow knifes, wallets, and blunt force hammers. It sells its products online. The company was founded in 2011 and is based in New York, New York.
