Solve LLC, formed in February of 2009 and based in Grinnell, Iowa, has developed proprietary software solutions for internet streaming of video content to TVs and mobile devices.  

Through mid-July 2010, Solve has created nine streaming "channels" for Roku (roku.com) and will launch further links to PopBox (popbox.com), Boxee (boxee.com) and Apple's devices (iPhone and iPad) in the next few weeks.
