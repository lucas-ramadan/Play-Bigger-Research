Prodigo Solutions, launched by healthcare supply chain professionals, is focused on driving real savings in the healthcare supply chain.  

Prodigo's suite of managed services and solutions are proven to drive contract compliance, automation, data quality and user adoption. Healthcare providers now have a viable and low-cost alternative for driving real savings.  Prodigo's suite of  SaaS solutions includes ProdigoBuyer (web-based requisitioning with workflow), ProdigoMarketplace (content management and punchout audit) and ProdigoXchange (B2B transaction services like EDI and fax/email purchase orders).
